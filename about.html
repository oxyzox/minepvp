Who We Are

MINEPVP was founded with the goal of creating a dynamic and friendly Minecraft community. We pride ourselves in being a platform for creativity, adventure, and exciting multiplayer experiences. Our journey has brought together players from all over India and beyond.

From PvP battles to custom modes, MINEPVP offers something for everyone. With a dedicated team, we ensure that our players always have something new and fun to explore. Join us and be part of our growing family!
